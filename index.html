Wanna know the story behind our film?

This film was recored for the Communication Lab Film Festival at New York University Abu Dhabi. Saira, the girl in the hijab lost her husband few months ago and is calling her husband's sister to console her. When Saira asks the sister of her husband to meet her, they both agree on that. Plot Twist!! The sister thinks that Saira is behind the death of her brother and the death wasn't simply a result of an accident. She wants the REVENGE. As she heads to Saira's place to kill her. BAM!! Another plot twist, turns out that Saira has a lesbian lover who would go to any extent to protect her. Thank you for all the positive feedback on the trailer.
